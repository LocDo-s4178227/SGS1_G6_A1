Đánh giá UX của Shopee Việt Nam / Shopee Vietnam UX Review

By Nguyễn Minh An · Published 18 July 2026 · 8 min read

Shopee dominates the Vietnamese e-commerce market with over 70% mobile traffic. This review examines how the platform balances aggressive promotional design with usable navigation for everyday shoppers.

Điểm mạnh (Strengths): The home screen prioritises flash sales and personalised recommendations without overwhelming first-time users. Category tabs are sticky, search autocomplete is fast, and product cards show price, rating, and sold count at a glance.

Điểm cần cải thiện (Areas for improvement): Checkout still requires too many taps on smaller screens. Voucher selection is buried two levels deep, and error states during payment rarely explain what went wrong in plain language.

Kết luận (Conclusion): Shopee delivers a polished mobile-first experience tuned for the Vietnamese market, but simplifying checkout and improving accessibility labels would benefit both casual buyers and power users.
